What's Hyperlocality?: Hyperlocal describes information oriented around a well-defined community with its primary focus directed toward the concerns of the population in that community. Hyperlocal websites can focus on very specialized topics (i.e., stories and issues of interest only to people in a very limited area).

About Me

I was born in New York City and live in Brooklyn. I work full-time at the intersection of clinical data science, health informatics, health data analytics, and health benefits administration. I use data to make data-driven models and dashboards that help other people (customers, colleagues, healthcare providers, business leaders) make better business-, finance-, and health-related decisions that impact organizations. I also work part-time in higher education which involves teaching the skills underlying the aforementioned areas (clinical science, experimental design, qualitative and quantitative analyses, etc.) to undergraduate and graduate students. I enjoy this framework because it allows me to go back and forth between theory (in higher education) and application (in industry). What I learn each day I try to apply to my own life and enjoy sharing insights that could benefit others.
